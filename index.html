I Shall Seal the Heavens

I Shall Seal the Heavens is the story of the young scholar Meng Hao, who gets forcibly recruited into a sect of immortal cultivators. In the cultivation world, the strong prey on the weak, and the law of the jungle prevails. Meng Hao must adapt to survive.

Characters

Meng Hao (孟浩) was once a young scholar in Yunjie Country, who had failed the imperial exams for the third time. The God is a transcendent cultivator who hails from the Immortal God Continent. Lord Fifth is a parrot and one of Meng Hao's closest companions along with the Meat Jelly. Wang Mu is one of the Chosens in the Ninth Mountain.
"Lord Fifth is a gentleman. He finds the act of screwing anything with fur or feathers inhumane and disgusting. However, because he is tied to Meng Hao, he has no choice but to carry out his requests, no matter how outrageous they might be."
— Lord Fifth's description of himself
